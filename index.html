Your Day-to-day at Hack Star

08:00 - 09:00

Start your day right with a healthy breakfast.

09:00 - 9:30

Class starts at 09:00. Don't be late.

09:30 - 10:30

Instructor led live coding session - Get the details for the days assignments and a walk through to make it all happen.

10:30 - 13:00

Take a short break if you want, but hurry! You'll work with a team mate, to follow the instructions presented by your instructor to build portfolio ready apps.

13:00 - 14:00

Time for lunch. Coding is hard work. Take a break.

14:00 - 15:00

Another live coding demonstration to introduce you to the key concepts that you'll need to continue learning.

15:00 - 18:00

Time to start coding another pair programming session.

18:30 - 19:30

Exercise! While not required, we suggest that you take a minute to stretch and exercise. It's an important part of being truly healthy and successful.

20:00 - 21:00

Let's eat shall we? A delicious, healthy dinner is exactly what you need after a long day of coding.

21:00 - 23:00

Now that you've got a good break and some energy, it's time to burn it off with some more coding before you go to bed.

23:15

Lights out. You can, of course, continue working in the classroom. But don't disturb those who are trying to rest.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the most commonly asked questions about the Hack Star program!

Do I need to know how to code before I join Hack Star?

Nope, absolutely not. But you will need to complete the pre-course work before you can come to the class.

Where is Hack Star located?

We are in Hyderabad. Located in Kondapur.

What are the sleeping arangements?

It's a boot camp, so the sleeping arrangements are dormitory / hostel style. You'll be sharing a room with 4-6 other people.

What do you mean by 'real client work'?

It means exactly that. You'll be working on real projects for real clients. This looks great on your resume!

What type of food do you serve?

The food is always healthy, nutritious, and delicious Indian food.
